Response to final question (5 points):

The sample will be normally distributed with an expected mean equal to the population mean, as it is a sample of the underlying population, if we assume sampling was random in nature. The samples distribution as N increases moves closer and closer toward a normal distribution, centered on 0 (zero) with a standard deviation of 1 (one).
